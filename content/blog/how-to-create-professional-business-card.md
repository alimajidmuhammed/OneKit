# How to Create a Professional Business Card in 5 Minutes

In today's digital-first world, having a professional business card is essential. Whether you're networking at events, attending conferences, or simply sharing your contact information, a well-designed business card leaves a lasting impression.

## Why Digital Business Cards Matter

Traditional paper cards get lost, damaged, or thrown away. Digital business cards offer several advantages:

- **Always accessible** - Available on any device
- **Easily shareable** - Send via email, WhatsApp, or social media
- **Eco-friendly** - No printing waste
- **Professional appearance** - Modern, customizable designs
- **Cost-effective** - No printing costs

## Pro Tips for Better Business Cards

### Keep It Simple

Don't overcrowd your card. Include only essential information:
- Name
- Job title
- Company
- 2-3 contact methods

### Use High-Quality LogosUpload your logo at the highest resolution possible (up to 5MB supported).

### Test QR Codes

If you're adding a QR code, make sure to test it with your phone camera before finalizing.

### Consider RTL for Arabic/Kurdish

OneKit supports Right-to-Left layouts for Arabic and Kurdish content. Use the "Sultan", "Hawler", or "Assyri" templates for perfect RTL support.
